## Save Our Wildlife

Simple wildlife conservation webpage.

### Files
- index.html
- styles.css  
- script.js
- wildlife.mp4 (add your video here)

### What it does
- Shows "Save Our Wildlife" heading
- Plays wildlife.mp4 video with controls
- Has description below video
- Button to hide/show and play video
- Works on mobile phones

### Setup
1. Add your wildlife.mp4 video file
2. Open index.html in browser
